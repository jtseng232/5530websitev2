Greenhills Lawnmowers

We are First Robotics Competition Team 5530.

Founded in 2014, we are a diverse team of students and mentors representing Greenhills School in Ann Arbor, Michigan, striving for excellence.

Awards

Team 5530 has won several awards (listed below).

  • 2015 FIM Howell Event: Rookie Inspiration Award
  • 2015 FIM Livonia Event: Rookie All Star Award
  • 2017 FIM Ann Arbor Pioneer Event: Innovation in Control Award
  • 2017 FIM Ann Arbor Pioneer Event: Event Finalist
  • 2017 FIM Howell Event: Event Finalist
